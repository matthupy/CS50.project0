Full Biography

Led by true-freshman quarterback Bo Nix and senior defensive lineman Derrick Brown, the Tigers finished 14th in the AP Poll with a record of 9-4, and 5-3 in the Western Division of the Southeastern Conference. They boasted wins over Oregon, Texas A&M, and in-state rival Alabama.
